About Bridge Travel Solutions

A trusted travel and relocation support platform based in Dubai with ground presence in Malawi, helping people access global opportunities through real guidance and experience.

Our Story

Bridge Travel Solutions was founded by an African traveler who personally experienced the challenges of moving abroad in search of better opportunities. From visa processes to job searching and adapting to new systems, the journey revealed how many people struggle without proper guidance. What began as informal help to others has now grown into a structured support platform based in Dubai, with active ground support in Malawi. Our purpose is simple — to make international travel, work, and study more accessible, clear, and trustworthy.

Mission & Vision
