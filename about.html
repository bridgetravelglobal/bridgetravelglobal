About Bridge Travel Solutions

A trusted travel and relocation support platform based in Dubai, guiding people from Africa and beyond to global opportunities.

Our Story

Bridge Travel Solutions was founded by an African traveler who experienced firsthand the challenges of moving across countries in search of better opportunities. From visa processes to job hunting and travel systems, the journey was not easy — and it revealed a bigger problem: many people lack trusted guidance. What started as personal experience helping others informally has now grown into a structured support platform based in Dubai. Today, we exist to simplify travel, study, and work abroad through real experience and honest guidance.
